First, select the rover that you choose from the orange dropdown or leave as is if you want the rover to be Curiosity. Each rover has its own set of images.

Secondly, select any camera from the blue dropdown to narrow down the search of the "Mars Image" or just leave "All Cameras" if you don't prefer any camera over another.

Lastly, select any date from the Calendar to search the image by the Earth date.

If the information is available the app will display a new image and its information in this text box. You can also click on the "See Image" button to see an enlarged version of the image in a new tab.
